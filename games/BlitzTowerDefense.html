? How to Play

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Stop balloons from reaching the end of the path. You have a limited number of lives. Survive all 60 waves to win!

🏗️ PLACING TOWERS

  • Click a tower in the sidebar to select it
  • Click any green tile on the map to place it (not on the path!)
  • Click a placed tower to upgrade or sell it
  • Each tower has 3 upgrade paths — max 5 tiers per path, but only 2 paths can be upgraded at once

⚔️ HEROES

  • Select your hero before the game from the Heroes tab
  • Place your hero on the map — only one hero allowed
  • Heroes level up by earning XP from kills, gaining new powers and unlocking abilities
  • Use hero abilities by clicking them in the sidebar (they have cooldowns!)

🌊 WAVES

  • Press Start Wave when ready — or enable Auto to start waves automatically
  • Earn cash from popping balloons and a bonus at the end of each wave
  • Farms and support towers generate income at the start of each wave
  • Bosses (MOAB, BFB, ZOMG, DDT, BAD, TITAN) appear from wave 20 onwards

⚡ POWERS

Single-use power-ups shown in the bar above the bottom controls. Click to activate instantly!

🧠 MONKEY KNOWLEDGE

Earn MK Points by completing waves. Spend them on permanent upgrades that carry over between games!
